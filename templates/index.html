About TiFi® Fire Entry Suit

TiFi® is a patented, indigenous fire entry suit tested at 84 kW/m² radiant heat flux and certified to EN ISO 11612 standards. It offers extended protection, eye shielding, and high-temperature resistance while remaining ergonomically designed for field use.

Key features

  • Protective for more than 3 minutes at 84 kW/m² heat flux
  • Eye protection from direct flame for 5 minutes
  • Hand gloves tested for 500°C contact heat
  • Biologically safe, non-carcinogenic materials
  • Buy-back facility after end of life
